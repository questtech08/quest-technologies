About Us

Quest Technologies is an innovative educational institute committed to equipping learners with the latest skills and knowledge in Industry 4.0. We offer a wide range of cutting-edge courses in IT programming, CAD, and digital transformation to meet the evolving demands of the modern workplace.

Our expert-led training programs emphasize practical learning and real-world applications, ensuring that students gain hands-on experience and industry-relevant skills. Join us to become a future-ready professional and stay ahead in the digital era.

A Vision Beyond Limits

"When I founded Quest Technologies, my vision was simple—to create a platform where knowledge meets innovation. Over the past 18 years, we have grown beyond expectations, collaborating with educational institutions, hosting workshops, and expanding into new domains. CaddCamm Solutions empowers mechanical, civil, and electrical engineers with industry-standard design tools like AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Revit, and more. Meanwhile, Global Bio Invention Centre is our leap into the world of biotechnology, focusing on Microbiology, Genomics, and Molecular Biology. Our journey is not just about technology; it is about the impact we make on students, professionals, and industries. The MoUs we have signed, the thousands of students we have trained, and the expertise we share are just the beginning. I invite you to be part of this ever-evolving journey. Quest Technologies is not just an institution—it is a movement toward a smarter future."

— Sudhakar, Founder
